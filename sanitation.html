Our Priority

Clean, Comfortable Nail Care

Clean tools, tidy stations, and client comfort are part of every appointment.

Tools are cleaned and sanitized between services, disposable items are used where appropriate, and manicure and pedicure areas are reset throughout the day to keep the salon fresh, organized, and welcoming.

✓

Sterilized Instruments

Every metal tool goes through a detailed heat-sterilization cycle between bookings.

✓

Tidy Stations

Our technicians completely wipe down, spray, and reset their desks after each customer.

✓

Client Comfort

Disposable liners are used for foot spas, and disposable buffers are opened fresh for you.
