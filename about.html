Our Journey

A Friendly neighborhood Salon

Blush & Buff Nail Studio is a friendly neighborhood nail salon in Richmond, Virginia, offering manicures, pedicures, gel polish, dip powder, acrylics, builder gel, and custom nail art.

Whether you are booking a quick polish refresh or a full set with detailed art, our goal is to make your visit comfortable, clean, and easy.

Blush & Buff Nail Studio is a friendly neighborhood nail salon focused on clean, comfortable services and polished results. From everyday manicures and relaxing pedicures to gel, dip powder, acrylics, builder gel, and custom nail art, our team helps clients feel put together for work, weekends, special occasions, and everything in between.

Our Commitment

Comfortable & Polished Results

We believe that visiting the nail salon should be a highlight of your week. Our environment is deliberately designed to feel clean, friendly, polished, and approachable—like a real neighborhood nail salon should.

By prioritizing welcoming customer care and high-quality product lines, our technicians provide precision work that looks incredible and lasts.
